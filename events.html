CipherQuest

In CipherQuest, participants embark on a fast-paced quest through multiple levels of binary puzzles, encryption–decryption challenges, algorithmic riddles, hidden QR clues, and logic-based number sequences. Each stage pushes your mental agility and technical understanding to the edge, demanding creativity, patience, and a deep curiosity for patterns and secrets.

Capture the Flag

The Capture The Flag (CTF) event is a thrilling cybersecurity competition that pushes participants to think critically, analyze deeply, and decode creatively. Designed for both beginners and enthusiasts, this challenge offers a perfect mix of technical puzzles, real-world security scenarios, and problem-solving excitement. This CTF challenge unfolds through three exciting levels, each crafted to test different dimensions of your cybersecurity knowledge and logical reasoning

Hackscape room

In Hack-Scape, teams are virtually (or physically) “locked” inside a simulated digital room filled with encrypted messages, hidden clues, and puzzling bugs. The objective? Debug, decrypt, and decode your way out before time runs out. Every solved riddle unlocks a new layer of the mystery, bringing you one step closer to the six-digit escape code that sets your team free.
